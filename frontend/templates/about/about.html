About MediLens

AI Powered Medicine Analysis Platform

Project Overview

MediLens combines EasyOCR, OpenCV and Gemini AI to analyze medicine packaging. Instead of manually searching medicine information online, users simply upload a medicine image and receive structured medicine details, AI-powered explanations and a counterfeit risk assessment within seconds.

Why MediLens?

  • Medicine labels are difficult to understand.
  • Counterfeit medicines are becoming increasingly common.
  • Patients often forget dosage instructions and medicine history.
  • Finding reliable medicine information online can be confusing.
  • MediLens provides all important information in one place.

Key Features

Medicine Scanner Upload medicine images for instant analysis.
OCR Extraction Reads medicine packaging using EasyOCR.
AI Medicine Detection Identifies medicine details using Gemini AI.
Medicine Explanation Explains medicines in patient-friendly language.
Counterfeit Analysis Evaluates packaging consistency and authenticity.
Medicine Shelf Save scanned medicines for future reference.
Medicine History Access previous medicine scans anytime.
Reminders Never miss important medication schedules.

How MediLens Works

  1. Upload a medicine image.
  2. Image preprocessing using OpenCV.
  3. OCR text extraction with EasyOCR.
  4. OCR cleaning and ranking.
  5. AI medicine identification using Gemini AI.
  6. Generate patient-friendly explanation.
  7. Perform counterfeit risk analysis.
  8. Store results in medicine history.
